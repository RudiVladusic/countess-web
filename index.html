Biography

The Countess was formed in February 2017 by keyboardist Bruno Delonga, vocalist Marija Marković and guitar player Marko Bešlić. After numerous changes of line-up in search of bass guitarist and drummer, in 2018 Martin Botica joins them as bass guitarist and Petar Radović takes place as drummer.

The Countess combines elements of doom, gothic, heavy and folk metal influenced by bands as Theatre Of Tragedy, Candlemass, Summoning, Embrio and fantasy and gothic literature and iconography as well.

The band is currently finishing up their first full-lenght release which will feature 9 new songs, new logo, album cover and whole new artwork. Release date is set in 2021 so stay tuned.
